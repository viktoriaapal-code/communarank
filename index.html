Your privacy is protected

CommunaRank collects only the information you provide in this form. Your name is displayed as you write it (e.g. "Anna M."). We never collect email addresses or contact details through reviews. No data is sold or shared with third parties. Reviews are stored locally in your browser and shared publicly only after moderation. CommunaRank complies with Swiss data protection law (nDSG) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
