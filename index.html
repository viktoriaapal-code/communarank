Our proprietary methodology for evaluating Swiss communities across 9 pillars

Methodology

The CommunaRank Smart Community Index aggregates publicly available Swiss federal data (OFS/FSO), cantonal statistics, OpenStreetMap infrastructure data, energy provider tariffs, and user-submitted data points. Each dimension is normalized to a 0–100 scale and weighted according to the user's stated priorities.
